Welcome! I'm a Portland, Oregon-based Product Manager, Coder-in-Training and Civic Tech Enthusiast. I’m currently using data and customer insights to help craft superb user experiences for Granicus products, all with an eye on increasing revenue and maximizing profitability.

About Me

I’ve been working in the Government Technology sector for 10 years. During that time I’ve served in multiple roles, which has allowed me to gain a thorough understanding of both customer, internal team, and business needs. Channeling this knowledge has enabled my success as a Product Manager for the last 8 years. Product Management can be a big umbrella covering many different business needs and I’ve done them all: Product Owner, Scrum Master, Trainer, Sales Engineer, User Researcher, Market Analyst, and even some Development Engineering.

When I’m not at work, you can find me at the ice rink for hockey, outside for a variety of activities like snowboarding, water skiing, camping and hiking, or at an event space for live music and comedy. I recently moved to Portland after living in California for most of my life and have been enjoying exploring my new city.
